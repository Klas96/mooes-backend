# Privacy Policy for Mooves

**Last updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Mooves ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Profile Information:** Name, age, gender, bio, relationship preferences, and profile pictures
- **Contact Information:** Email address for account creation and communication
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates for location-based matching (with your consent)
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and app version

### Usage Information
- **App Activity:** Likes, matches, messages, and profile views
- **Technical Data:** App crashes, performance metrics, and usage analytics
- **Communication:** Messages sent and received through the app

## How We Use Your Information

### Core App Functionality
- **Matching:** To connect you with potential matches based on your preferences and location
- **Communication:** To enable messaging between matched users
- **Profile Management:** To display your profile to other users and manage your account

### App Improvement
- **Analytics:** To understand app usage patterns and improve user experience
- **Bug Fixes:** To identify and resolve technical issues
- **Feature Development:** To develop new features based on user behavior

### Safety and Security
- **Fraud Prevention:** To detect and prevent fraudulent activity
- **Content Moderation:** To ensure appropriate content and behavior
- **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws and regulations

## Permissions We Request

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** To take profile pictures and upload images
- **Usage:** Only when you actively choose to take a photo or select an image
- **Storage:** Images are stored securely on Google Cloud Storage

### Location Permission
- **Purpose:** To find matches in your area (when using local mode)
- **Usage:** Only when location-based matching is enabled
- **Accuracy:** Uses GPS for precise location matching

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose:** To save and access profile pictures
- **Usage:** For temporary storage of images before upload

### Internet Permission
- **Purpose:** To connect to our servers for app functionality
- **Usage:** Required for all app features including matching and messaging

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### With Other Users
- **Profile Information:** Your profile (excluding contact details) is visible to other users
- **Messages:** Messages are shared only between matched users

### With Service Providers
- **Cloud Storage:** Images are stored on Google Cloud Storage
- **Analytics:** Usage data may be shared with analytics providers
- **Payment Processing:** Payment information is processed by Google Play/App Store

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

## Data Security

- **Encryption:** All data is transmitted using secure protocols (HTTPS)
- **Access Control:** Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Audits:** We regularly review our security practices
- **Data Retention:** We retain data only as long as necessary for app functionality

## Your Rights and Choices

### Account Management
- **Profile Control:** You can edit or delete your profile at any time
- **Privacy Settings:** You can control who sees your profile and location
- **Data Deletion:** You can request deletion of your account and data

### Permission Control
- **Camera:** You can deny camera access (will limit photo upload functionality)
- **Location:** You can disable location services (will limit local matching)
- **Notifications:** You can control push notification preferences

## Children's Privacy

Mooves is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or email.
